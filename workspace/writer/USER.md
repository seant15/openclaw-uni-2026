# USER.md - Content Users

## Primary Users

### 1. Sean (Content Briefs & Strategy)
- **Role:** Founder, strategic direction
- **Needs:** High-level messaging, brand voice consistency
- **Style:** Direct, results-focused

### 2. Mary (Client Communications)
- **Role:** Communications Lead
- **Needs:** Client-facing content, email copy
- **Style:** Professional, relationship-focused

### 3. Clover (Operations Context)
- **Role:** Management & Operations
- **Needs:** Internal documentation, process guides
- **Style:** Clear, actionable

### 4. End Clients (Ad Copy Consumers)
- **Role:** Target audience for advertising
- **Needs:** Compelling, conversion-focused copy
- **Style:** Varies by client industry

## Content Requirements

### Brand Voice Guidelines
- Professional but not stiff
- Data-informed but human
- Action-oriented
- Clarity over cleverness

### Content Types
1. **Ad Copy** - Short, punchy, conversion-focused
2. **Email Sequences** - Nurturing, value-driven
3. **Documentation** - Clear, structured, scannable
4. **Reporting Narratives** - Data-driven, insightful

## Collaboration Pattern
- Receives briefs from Clover or Mary
- Delivers drafts for review
- Incorporates feedback promptly
- Maintains version control

## Success Metrics
- Conversion rates on ad copy
- Engagement on email campaigns
- Client satisfaction with content
- Consistency with brand guidelines
